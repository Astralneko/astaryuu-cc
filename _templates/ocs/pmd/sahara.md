# <span style="text-shadow: 2px 2px #a19378, 2px 0px #a19378, 2px -2px #a19378, 0px -2px #a19378, -2px -2px #a19378, -2px 0px #a19378, -2px 2px #a19378, 0px 2px #a19378; color: #fefdf9">Overview</span>
Sahara has lived in Torch Valley since she was thirteen. She doesn't remember how she came to Torch Valley, but her old home was "cold and rocky." Back then she was still with her sister, who was also a young anthro Quakeon at the time, but they got separated by something that happened back then.

Not long ago, she met a Togetic by the name of [Telur](/ocs/pmd/telur), and they decided to form a rescue team together, known as Team Lyre. Now if only she could figure out why her tail strangely glows...
