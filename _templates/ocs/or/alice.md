# Overview
Alice is from another dimension - one she calls "The Void." Namely since quite literally nothing else is in there. She is occasionally referred to in Avalonia as the "Shooting Star Girl," due to falling from the sky and onto Ashenville, destroying much of the village in the process.

Alice's special powers are void-based in nature. She uses void energy for a couple of skills - firing a beam (or shockwave) of void energy to deal damage or using a damage trade ability that heals one at the expense of herself. She also uses this energy to go into her "Abyss Star Form" or simply use the wings and/or the scythe, the latter she usually prefers to the beams of void energy. When her power starts to run out, especially due to damage trading, Alice appears to weigh almost nothing, making her easy to carry.

But perhaps most special is her ability to link to a regular being - a trait shared among ultradimensional beings such as herself. Alice can "link" to certain people and then can phase into them, giving them a fraction of her power (since all the power would be a one-way ticket to the void). She may also partially unphase in order to talk to the linked person in private. Only people that are linked to Alice can use her scythe; even if one manages to lift it without Alice, trying to use it will cause void feedback - in other words, the person that did it would disappear.

# Abilities
## Projection
As she is a fairy, Alice's true form is small, about 7 inches (comparable to [Pyra](/ocs/other/pyra/)). However, Alice can "project" a larger form, which can also be used to present as human or as some other species.
The projection is, for all intents and purposes, physical. However, the projection has no mass of its own, making it easy to pick up and throw around Alice.

## Connection
Alice can "connect" or "link" to any sentient being, described as a "non-invasive possession". When she links to someone, she loses her physical form, but can appear in the vision of the possessed being.
