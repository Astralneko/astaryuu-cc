* At a huge 9' 9" from toe to ear tip, Lilith is my tallest character, at least of those that are anthropomorphic (the body type, not just being a furry).
* Since she's revived as a beast and not simply a ghost, Lilith's body ages, just slower than usual. She's around 25 in terms of biological age, at which point the difference in the two rates increases significantly.
