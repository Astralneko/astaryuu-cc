The homepage for all things Lutera, my fandom world (universe? multiverse? cosmere? idk what to call it).

Overview

Lutera and Laniakea are twin worlds, effectively. It's been worked on off-and-on since 2016, and was actually relevant for me getting into art in the first place.

Below you will see the regions of Lutera. Each has its own homepage, especially Laniakea as it is so vast.

Verela

Initially created in 2016, Verela was the first fan region I created. It has seen a considerable revamp since it was first developed though, as in around 2021 I revived the Verela project for the purposes of making a fangame based on it.

Zantaru

Initially created in 2017 or 2018, Zantaru was the second fan region I created. It has seen a considerable revamp since it was first developed though, as I stopped working on it and Verela in 2018 or 2019 due to stupid decisions, only to come back to both later on in 2021 or so.

Silati

Initially created in 2020, Silati was the fifth fan region I created. It was originally created during work on another region, Syraik, but has since subsumed Syraik's role in terms of development since Texas's governor is an idiot. Syraik, on the other hand, was also initially created in 2018.

Xureen

Initially created in 2019, Xureen was the fourth fan region I created. It's the only one that hasn't had a significant revamp in its history (including Laniakea), as the idea of a Australia-based region entirely underground is just Too Cool.

Tzarin

Initially created in 2022, Tzarin was the sixth fan region I created. The whole idea came from me wanting to make the Champion of this such region a superboss in my Verela-based fangame lmao.

Laniakea

Initially created in 2017, before receiving a revamp in 2019, before receiving a revamp in 2020, before receiving a revamp in 2021, Laniakea is a Mystery Dungeon-esque world, albeit set in a somewhat modern society as compared to typical PMD fare.
