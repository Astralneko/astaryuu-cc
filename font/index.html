who is

Largely due to being a conlanger, there are several fonts I have produced. Chief among them is the pixel font Truth and Ideals, used throughout this website. It is intended to support every single one of my conlangs at once.

If you prefer less pixelated fonts, AH Rokajang Shika and AH Kardon Standard are also there. They use the same codepoints as Truth and Ideals, so you can use it as a backup.

For the record, fonts of mine generally start with "AH" because I used to produce fonts on Fontstruct under the username "Alexherobrine45." As Truth and Ideals is directly based on a font from a video game, it is an exception.
