# <span style="text-shadow: 2px 2px #efefef, 2px 0px #efefef, 2px -2px #efefef, 0px -2px #efefef, -2px -2px #efefef, -2px 0px #efefef, -2px 2px #efefef, 0px 2px #efefef; color: #84ffd5">Overview</span>
Junko is a robobun, a model of robotic home assistant or maid created by a Japanese company. Her specific model was slated for deprecation, but she was rescued by an old man named Gerald, who fixed her up and installed laser weaponry. Now, Junko works for SecuROM under [Lovely](/ocs/tafo/lovely/).
