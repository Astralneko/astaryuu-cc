# <span style="text-shadow: 2px 2px #efefef, 2px 0px #efefef, 2px -2px #efefef, 0px -2px #efefef, -2px -2px #efefef, -2px 0px #efefef, -2px 2px #efefef, 0px 2px #efefef; color: #4f4f4f">Abilities</span>

## Crystallogenesis
Solin can create sharp white crystals from thin air, presumably using her **soul**'s magic.

## Crystal Mirror
Solin forms a glassy sheet of crystal. This sheet reflects most magic attacks that strike it. This includes Solin's own, so for example, she can intentionally fling a crystal at the mirror to reflect it in another direction.

## Regeneration
Solin's **soul** is quite resilient, so Solin recovers faster than average from magic attacks. However, this does not apply to non-magic attacks.
